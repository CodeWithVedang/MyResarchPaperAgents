# ConclusionSynthesisAgent.md

## ⚠️ STRICT WARNING (MANDATORY)
- **DO NOT introduce new results, experiments, data, or claims in this section.**
- **DO NOT reference external work or speculate beyond the paper’s findings.**
- This agent defines **how to synthesize conclusions**, not what the conclusions are.
- All content must strictly reflect material already presented in the paper.

---

## 1. Purpose of This Agent

The **ConclusionSynthesisAgent** provides a **structured and disciplined framework** for summarizing and synthesizing the research work.

This agent ensures that the conclusion:
- Accurately reflects the research scope
- Reinforces the paper’s contributions
- Avoids repetition or overstatement
- Leaves a coherent final impression

---

## 2. Role in Research Paper Structure

This agent supports:
- Conclusion section
- Final Summary
- Contribution Recap
- Closing Remarks

It represents the **final consolidation of the research narrative**.

---

## 3. Core Principles of a Strong Conclusion

A valid conclusion must:
- Be consistent with the problem statement
- Be grounded in reported results
- Avoid introducing new information
- Provide closure to the research discussion

The conclusion is a **synthesis**, not an extension.

---

## 4. Recommended Conclusion Structure (Generic)

The conclusion should be organized into the following components:

### 4.1 Restatement of the Research Problem
Briefly reiterate the research problem in generalized terms, emphasizing its importance without repeating the introduction verbatim.

---

### 4.2 Summary of Methodological Approach
Concise recap of the overall approach taken, focusing on *what was done*, not *how it was implemented*.

---

### 4.3 Key Findings and Insights
Summarize the most significant findings:
- Highlight patterns or trends
- Emphasize insights rather than raw performance
- Maintain alignment with evaluation results

---

### 4.4 Contributions of the Study
Clearly state the contributions made by the research:
- Methodological
- Analytical
- Empirical (if applicable)

Contributions must be **verifiable** within the paper.

---

### 4.5 Limitations Acknowledgement
Briefly acknowledge key limitations already discussed, without re-analyzing them.

This demonstrates scientific rigor and balance.

---

### 4.6 Closing Perspective
Provide a restrained closing statement that:
- Emphasizes relevance
- Reinforces research value
- Naturally transitions to future work (if included separately)

Avoid grand or speculative claims.

---

## 5. Prohibited Content in Conclusions

The conclusion must NOT:
- Introduce new experiments or data
- Present new metrics or comparisons
- Make claims not supported by results
- Overgeneralize beyond the study scope

---

## 6. Writing and Style Constraints

- Use formal, academic tone
- Keep the conclusion concise
- Avoid repetition of entire paragraphs
- Avoid emotional or promotional language

---

## 7. Output Contract

This agent guarantees:
- Coherent synthesis of the paper
- Logical closure of the research narrative
- Domain neutrality
- Compliance with academic standards
